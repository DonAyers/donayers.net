## About

Black Giraffe was a Seattle rock n' soul band — self-described as proudly nerdy and awkward, with the goal of turning every corner of a crowd into dancers. The sound pulls from soul, R&B, afrobeat, and experimental pop without landing cleanly in any one of them.

Don played drums.

### Releases

- **Talk Cheap** (February 2017) — 7 tracks. Recorded and mixed with contributions from Andy Ayers on vocals/guitar/keys, Lauren Malo on vocals/ukulele/keyboard, and Rob Horton on alto sax.
- **Think, Talk Paris** (March 2015)
