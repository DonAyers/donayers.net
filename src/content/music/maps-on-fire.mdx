## About

Maps on Fire was a Seattle-based indie rock project active through the early 2010s. The sound leaned on clean melodic guitar work and dynamic song structures — quiet passages that opened up into dense, layered arrangements.

### Releases

- **After** (2010) — 11-track full-length. Atmospheric and patient, with plenty of room to breathe.
- **A Dot An Arrow** (2011) — 4-track EP. Tighter, more focused writing. Includes *Autophobia*, *Black Flowers*, and *Cabin Fever*.
- **For What Its Worth** (2012) — 2-track release. *Alight* and *My Dear*.
- **Control EP** — Additional EP release.
