## About the Project

**Neon Dreams** is a 5-track EP exploring the sonic landscape of a cyberpunk future. Inspired by blade runner aesthetics and retro-futuristic synth sounds, this project blends warm analog tones with modern production techniques.

### Tracklist

1. **Digital Rain** (4:32) - Opening with atmospheric pads and driving arpeggios
2. **Chrome City** (5:18) - Upbeat synthwave with punchy drums
3. **Midnight Protocol** (6:45) - Dark and moody ambient exploration
4. **Neon Pulse** (4:55) - High-energy track with vocoded vocals
5. **Dawn Sequence** (7:20) - Ethereal closer with layered synths

### Production Notes

All tracks were produced using a combination of hardware synthesizers (Moog Subsequent 37, Korg Minilogue XD) and software instruments (Serum, Omnisphere). Mixed and mastered in Ableton Live.
