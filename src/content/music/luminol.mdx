## About

Luminol was a Seattle-based experimental rock project that mixed ambient textures with progressive song structures and live instrumentation. The approach was wide-open — closer to a soundtrack sensibility than a straight rock band.

Don played drums and acoustic guitar.

### Releases

- **Locked In The Woods** (August 2011) — 6 tracks. Vocals by Barbara Burgio, guitars by Mike Shanks, bass by Robbie Houston, violin by Juan Quiroga.
  - *Intro / Right Hand Man / Squeaking Soul / Grapes / Too Late / Water on Water*
- **Organ Grinder** (May 2009) — 2 tracks. *Snarling Ghost* and *Symbiotic Disharmony*.
- **Hon'ne. Tatemae.** — Unmastered release.
